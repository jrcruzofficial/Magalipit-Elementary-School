About School

Magalipit Elementary School is one of the identified Last Mile Schools in Montalban, Rizal,
and is located in the quiet and close-knit community of Barangay Mascap. It is a complete elementary school,
catering to learners from Kindergarten to Grade Six, and has consistently served as a second home for children in the area.
With an average enrollment of 200–250 learners, the school remains small in size but rich in community spirit and dedication.
Despite not having the same level of funding or large facilities commonly found in bigger schools,
Magalipit Elementary School continues to uphold its commitment to providing quality, learner-centered education.
The school maximizes the resources it has and ensures that every learner receives proper attention, guidance,
and support—something that smaller schools are able to offer more personally.
Currently, Magalipit ES is staffed by 8 nationally funded teachers and 2 non-teaching personnel,
led by an Assistant School Principal II and supported by an Administrative Officer II.
Each member of the teaching and non-teaching staff works collaboratively to maintain a nurturing and inclusive learning environment,
ensuring that learners not only gain academic skills but also develop values that will guide them in their future endeavors.
Magalipit Elementary School may be modest in size, but its heart for service, dedication to learners,
and strong connection with the community make it a meaningful and impactful institution—proving
that a school does not need to be big or highly funded to deliver quality education.
